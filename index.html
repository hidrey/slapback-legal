Privacy Policy

Overview

SlapBack is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Everything stays on your device.

Data We Collect

None. SlapBack does not collect any personal information, usage data, analytics, or telemetry. There are no accounts, no sign-ups, and no tracking.

Sensor Data

SlapBack accesses your device's accelerometer and motion sensors to detect physical interactions (slaps, drops, orientation changes). This data is processed entirely on your device in real time and is never recorded, stored, or transmitted anywhere.

Microphone

SlapBack may request microphone access for the Blow Mode feature. Audio is processed locally in real time and is never recorded, stored, or transmitted.

Purchases

In-app purchases are handled entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not have access to your payment information.

Third-Party Services

SlapBack does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Data Storage

Your preferences (sound pack selection, sensitivity settings, slap statistics) are stored locally on your device using standard iOS storage. This data is not accessible to us or any third party.

Children's Privacy

SlapBack does not collect any data from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
